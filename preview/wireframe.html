01 - Context

What is Vue?

Vue is a SOI (Satellite Object Identification) Matching Tool used by the Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance Division (ISRD) at Vandenberg Air Force Base. The tool optimizes and tasks available ground- and space-based sensors for intelligence collection, improving data accuracy for analysis and mission planning.

CSpOC is the U.S.-led multinational space operations center responsible for command and control of space forces - tracking over 20,000 objects in orbit.

World map - sensor coverage visualization

Global sensor network: ground-based radar sites tracking LEO and GEO satellites

02 - Problem

A Manual Process With High Stakes

Every week, Collection Managers manually built "decks" - matching ~1,500 priority space objects to available radar sensors. The process relied on Excel spreadsheets, raw TLE data files, and institutional knowledge.

The result: bad collects - where sensors were tasked to observe objects that fell outside their viewing range - ran as high as 18–35%. Each failure meant wasted collection time and gaps in space situational awareness.

03 - Process

Understanding the Mission First

Designing for a classified intelligence environment meant deep domain learning. I conducted user interviews with Collection Managers to understand the weekly tasking workflow, failure modes, and the physical constraints of radar sensors.

04 - Solution

Clarity at Every Step of the Deck

Vue guides Collection Managers through a structured 3-step workflow: Input Data → Review Matches → Special Instructions. The simplest, most impactful design decision was applying quality color labels - Optimal, Baseline, Suboptimal - directly to each match row.

05 - Impact

Results Briefed to the CSpOC Commander

Vue's impact was measurable and immediate. Within the first operating period, bad collect rates dropped to near zero - a result significant enough to be briefed directly to Col Delauter, the CSpOC Commander.

"Using Vue has saved the team a total of 100 man-hours per week."

- TSgt Wibiral, ISRD / CSpOC, Primary User

Accuracy

Bad collects reduced from 18–35% to 0–3%, virtually eliminating wasted sensor time.

Efficiency

100 man-hours saved per week - freeing analysts for higher-value intelligence work.

Adoption

Vue became the primary tool for all ISRD SOI tasking needs at Vandenberg AFB.
